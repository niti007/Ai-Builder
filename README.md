# 🚀 AI Builder

**Got something you want to ship?** AI Builder connects you with the right collaborators to build it.

Whether it's a side project, a SaaS idea, or just a concept in your head — we match you with the perfect co-founder, collaborator, or just a good chat partner to bring your vision to life.

## 🎯 What We Do

AI Builder is a matchmaking platform for builders, creators, and entrepreneurs. We use AI-powered matching to connect you with the right people to collaborate on your next big idea.

### How It Works

**Option 1: Get AI-Curated Matches**
- Receive one perfectly matched collaborator per week
- Handpicked by our AI based on your project and goals
- Start conversations and build together

**Option 2: Browse All Builders**
- Explore our community of makers, entrepreneurs, and creators
- Find someone who shares your vision
- Initiate conversations directly
- Network and collaborate on your terms

**Option 3: You Decide the Collab**
- Co-founder looking for a technical co-founder
- Startup founder seeking a designer or marketer
- Just want to chat and exchange ideas
- You control what type of collaboration you're looking for

## ✨ Key Features

- 🤖 **AI-Powered Matching** — Smart algorithm matches you with the right collaborators based on your project and goals
- 🔍 **Browse Builders** — Explore our community of creators, founders, and makers
- 💬 **Start Conversations** — Connect directly with potential collaborators
- 📈 **34 Builders, 12 Matched This Week** — Growing community of makers shipping projects
- 🎯 **Flexible Collaboration** — Find co-founders, collaborators, mentors, or just network
- ⚡ **Quick Matching** — Get new matches weekly without the hassle

## 🌍 Use Cases

✅ **Side Project** — Find someone to build with on nights and weekends

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Sign Up** — Create your builder profile
2. **Describe Your Project** — Tell us what you want to ship
3. **Get Matched** — Receive AI-curated matches weekly, or browse all builders
4. **Connect** — Start conversations and find your perfect collaborator
5. **Build** — Ship something great together
